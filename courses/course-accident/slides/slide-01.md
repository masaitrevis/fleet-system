# Accident Prevention

## The Reality of Accidents

Most accidents are **preventable** through proper training, vehicle maintenance, and safe driving practices.

### Statistics
- Speeding is a factor in 30% of fatal crashes
- Distracted driving causes 9 deaths daily
- Fatigue-related crashes often result in serious injury

## Prevention Strategies

### Driver Training
- Complete all required training courses
- Attend refresher training annually
- Learn from near-miss incidents

### Vehicle Maintenance
- Follow all DVIR requirements
- Report defects immediately
- Keep maintenance records current

### Speed Management
- Obey posted speed limits
- Reduce speed in adverse conditions
- Allow extra time for trips

### Distraction Avoidance
- No phone use while driving
- Set GPS before departure
- Pull over for complex tasks

### Fatigue Management
- Follow HOS regulations
- Take required breaks
- Get adequate sleep (7-8 hours)

## The Cost of Accidents

- Human lives and injuries
- Vehicle damage and downtime
- Increased insurance premiums
- Legal liability
- Company reputation damage
