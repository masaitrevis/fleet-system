# If an Accident Occurs

## Immediate Actions

### 1. Stop Immediately
- Pull over to a safe location if possible
- Turn on hazard lights
- Set up warning triangles

### 2. Secure the Scene
- Prevent further accidents
- Keep traffic moving if possible
- Protect evidence

### 3. Check for Injuries
- Check yourself first
- Check passengers and other drivers
- Call emergency services if anyone is hurt

### 4. Do Not Admit Fault
- Be polite and cooperative
- Do not say "I'm sorry" or admit blame
- Do not make promises or agreements

### 5. Document Everything
- Take photos of all vehicles involved
- Get witness names and contact info
- Note weather and road conditions
- Sketch the accident scene

### 6. Notify Your Supervisor
- **Within 1 hour** of the accident
- Provide location and brief description
- Follow company procedures

## Required Reports

Complete all required documentation:
- Police report (if required)
- Company accident report
- Insurance claim forms
- Any regulatory reports
