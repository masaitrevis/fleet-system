# Fatigue Warning Signs

## Recognizing Driver Fatigue

Know these warning signs in yourself and others:

### Physical Signs
- Frequent yawning or blinking
- Heavy eyelids
- Difficulty keeping head up
- Rubbing eyes repeatedly

### Cognitive Signs
- Difficulty concentrating
- Memory lapses (missing exits)
- Slow reaction times
- Poor decision making

### Driving Behavior
- Missing traffic signs or exits
- Drifting between lanes
- Tailgating unintentionally
- Inconsistent speed

## The Microsleep Danger

A microsleep is a brief, unintended episode of sleep:
- Can last from a fraction of a second to 30 seconds
- Driver is essentially unconscious
- At 60 km/h, 5 seconds = 83 meters traveled blind

## When to Stop

**STOP DRIVING immediately if you experience:**
- Any of the warning signs above
- Nodding off or head dropping
- Can't remember the last few minutes
- Eyes closing involuntarily

Find a safe place to rest before continuing.
