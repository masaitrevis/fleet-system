# Hours of Service Regulations

## Overview

Hours of Service (HOS) regulations are designed to prevent driver fatigue and ensure road safety.

## Key Limits

### Daily Driving Limit
- **11 hours** maximum driving time
- After **10 consecutive hours off-duty**

### On-Duty Window
- **14-hour** window from start of duty
- Includes driving, loading, inspections

### Weekly Limits
- **60 hours** in 7 days
- **70 hours** in 8 days

### Break Requirements
- **30-minute break** after 8 hours of driving
- Must be off-duty or in sleeper berth

## Why HOS Matters

- Fatigue is a factor in 30% of commercial vehicle accidents
- Violations result in fines and penalties
- Company liability increases with violations
- Driver health and safety are protected

## Record Keeping

All driving hours must be documented in:
- Electronic Logging Device (ELD) - preferred
- Paper logbook (if ELD exempt)
