# Scanning the Road

## The Importance of Scanning

Effective scanning is the foundation of defensive driving. You need to be aware of everything happening around your vehicle.

## Proper Scanning Pattern

### Look Ahead (12-15 seconds)
- **City driving**: 1-1.5 blocks ahead
- **Highway driving**: 1/4 mile ahead
- Watch for brake lights, turn signals, hazards

### Check Mirrors (Every 5-8 seconds)
- Rear-view mirror: Check traffic behind you
- Side mirrors: Monitor vehicles in adjacent lanes
- Before any maneuver: Check all mirrors

### Scan Intersections
- Look left, right, and left again before entering
- Check for pedestrians and cyclists
- Watch for vehicles running red lights

## Identify Escape Routes

Always have a "way out" planned:
- Know where you can pull over if needed
- Identify open lanes for emergency maneuvers
- Watch for shoulder space on highways

## Check Blind Spots

- Always check blind spots before:
  - Changing lanes
  - Merging
  - Turning
  - Backing up
