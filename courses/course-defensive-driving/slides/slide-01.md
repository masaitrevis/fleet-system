# Introduction to Defensive Driving

## Course Overview

Defensive driving is a set of driving skills that allows you to defend yourself against possible collisions caused by bad drivers, drunk drivers, and poor weather.

## Key Principles

- **Stay alert and focused** - Always pay attention to your surroundings
- **Anticipate hazards** - Look for potential dangers before they become problems
- **Maintain safe following distance** - Keep enough space to react
- **Adapt to road conditions** - Adjust your driving based on weather and traffic

## Why Defensive Driving Matters

- Reduces accident risk by up to 50%
- Protects you from other drivers' mistakes
- Saves lives and prevents injuries
- Lowers insurance costs and vehicle damage

## Course Objectives

By the end of this course, you will:
1. Understand the principles of defensive driving
2. Apply the 3-second rule effectively
3. Scan the road properly for hazards
4. Manage distractions while driving
