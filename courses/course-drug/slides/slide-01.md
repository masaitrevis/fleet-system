# Drug & Alcohol Policy

## Zero Tolerance Policy

Our company maintains a **strict zero-tolerance policy** for drugs and alcohol.

## Prohibited Activities

### Alcohol
- No alcohol within **8 hours** of duty
- No alcohol while on duty
- No alcohol in company vehicles

### Drugs
- No illegal drug use
- No misuse of prescription medications
- No cannabis use (even where legal)

### Testing
- Pre-employment screening
- Random testing throughout employment
- Post-accident testing (mandatory)
- Reasonable suspicion testing

## Prescription Medications

If taking prescription medications:
- Report to supervisor
- Provide doctor's note if needed
- Do not drive if medication affects alertness
- Keep medications in original containers

## Consequences of Violations

Violations result in:
- **Immediate suspension**
- Mandatory drug/alcohol testing
- Disciplinary action up to termination
- DOT reporting (if applicable)
- Loss of driving privileges

## Getting Help

If you have a substance abuse problem:
- Contact HR confidentially
- Company may provide assistance resources
- Self-reporting may protect your job
