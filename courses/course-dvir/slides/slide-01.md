# Introduction to DVIR

## What is DVIR?

The Daily Vehicle Inspection Report (DVIR) is a **legal requirement** for commercial vehicle operators.

## Purpose of DVIR

- Ensure vehicle safety before operation
- Document defects found during inspection
- Prove compliance with safety regulations
- Protect drivers and the company
- Create maintenance records

## When to Complete DVIR

### Pre-Trip Inspection
- **Before starting** each workday
- Verify vehicle is safe to operate
- Check all critical systems

### Post-Trip Inspection
- **At the end** of each workday
- Document any defects found
- Ensure next driver is informed

## Legal Requirements

DVIR is required by law for:
- Commercial vehicles over 4,500 kg
- Vehicles carrying hazardous materials
- All company fleet vehicles

## Consequences of Non-Compliance

- Fines and penalties
- Vehicle taken out of service
- Company liability in accidents
- Driver disciplinary action
