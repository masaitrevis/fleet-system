# Common Defects to Check

## Critical Defects (Out of Service)

These defects mean the vehicle **cannot be operated**:

### Brake Defects
- Air leaks in brake system
- Worn brake pads (under 1/4 inch)
- Brake warning lights on
- Spongy or unresponsive brake pedal

### Tire Issues
- Tread depth under 2/32 inch
- Bulges, cuts, or visible cord
- Mismatched tire sizes on same axle
- Under-inflated tires

### Steering Defects
- Excessive play in steering wheel
- Power steering failure
- Loose or missing steering components

### Lighting Problems
- Non-functional headlights
- Missing brake lights
- Inoperative turn signals

## Suspension Issues
- Broken or missing springs
- Loose U-bolts
- Air suspension leaks

## Never Operate With Critical Defects

Report immediately and request alternate vehicle.
