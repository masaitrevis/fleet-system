# Pre-Trip Inspection

## Systematic Inspection Order

Follow this order every time for thoroughness:

### 1. Engine Compartment
- Oil level and condition
- Coolant level
- Belt condition and tension
- Hose condition (no cracks or leaks)
- Battery connections

### 2. Front of Vehicle
- Headlights (high and low beam)
- Turn signals
- Windshield condition (no cracks)
- Windshield wipers and fluid
- License plate visibility

### 3. Driver Side
- Tire condition and pressure
- Wheel lug nuts
- Fuel tank and cap
- Mirrors (clean and secure)

### 4. Rear of Vehicle
- Brake lights
- Turn signals
- Cargo securement
- Tailgate/liftgate operation

### 5. Passenger Side
- Repeat driver side checks

### 6. Inside Cab
- Gauges working
- Horn
- Seatbelts
- Fire extinguisher present
- Emergency triangles present

## Documentation

Record any defects on the DVIR form immediately.
